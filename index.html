Alcoholism

Alcoholism or alcohol use disorder is a condition in which a person has a desire or physical need to consume alcohol, even though it has a negative impact on their life. It affects about 15 million people a year in the us. There are also about 88 thousand alcohol related deaths a year. More than 7 percent of alcohol drnkers become addicted. Alcohol use disorder can not only cause health problems but much like other substances can cause withdrawls.People who suffer from this disorder have a life expectancy of 47-53 years for men and 50-58 years for women.
