Science facts
Short term effects: Many of these long term effects are common but unfortunately, there are those who don't know about most of them. Along with the effects of long term drinking, there are effects that can even happen during one night of binge drinking
  • Slurred speech
  • Black outs
  • Anemia
  • Diarrhea
  • Vomiting
  • Nausea
  • Drowsiness
  • Impaired judgment
  • Being uncoordinated
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Distorted vision
  • Unconsciousness
Long term effects:Over time, lots of organized data has emerged from legitimate research involving alcoholism. Long term effects such as these have been discovered:
  • High blood pressure, stroke or other heart conditions
  • Liver disease
  • Nerve damage
  • Permanent brain damage
  • Vitamin b1 deficiency (which can lead to amnesia or disorientation)
  • Ulcers
  • Cancer of the mouth
  • Cancer of the throat
  • Unintentional injuries
  • Malnutrition
